BLOCK 1 – APTITUDE (Speed, Time & Distance)

Ques-1

A train travels 60 km in 1 hour. What is its speed in m/s?

Solution:

        Formula;
               
             ( 1 km/h = 5/18 m/s )

      = 60 * 5 / 18
      = 16.67  m/s

Answer: 16.67 m/s.

Ques-2

A car travels at 40 km/h for 2 hours and 60 km/h for next 2 hours.
Find average speed.

Solution:

            Formula;
                    average speed = Total Distance / Total Time

            Distance;
                    D1 = 40 * 2 = 80 km
                    D2 = 60 * 2 = 120 km

            Total Distance = 200 km
            Total time = 4 hrs 
             

             average speed = 200 / 4 = 50 

Answer: 50 km/h.


Ques-3

Two trains moving in opposite directions at 50 km/h and 70 km/h.
Find relative speed.

Solution:

        relative speed = 50 + 70
                        = 120

Answer : 120 km/h.


Ques-4

A person walks at 5 km/h and runs at 10 km/h.
Find time taken to cover 15 km if half distance is walked and half is run.

Solution:

        Total Distance = 15 km 
        half part = 7.5 km

        Walking time = 7.5 / 5 = 1.5
        Running time = 7.5 / 10 = 0.75 

        Total time = 1.5 + 0.75
                   = 2.25

Answer: 2 hrs 15 min.

Ques-5

If a train crosses a pole in 10 seconds and its length is 100 meters,
find its speed.

Solution:


        Formula;
                Speed = Distance / Time

                      = 100 / 10 

                      = 10

Answer: 10 m/s.
