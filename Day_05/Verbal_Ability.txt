BLOCK 2 – VERBAL ABILITY (Advanced)

Reading Passage

Algorithms play a crucial role in optimizing system performance.
Efficient algorithms reduce execution time and resource consumption.

Questions

1. Why are algorithms important?

Answer: 

       (*) Algorithms are important because they optimize system performance by 
           reducing execution time and resource usage.


2. What happens if inefficient algorithms are used?

Answer:

        (*) They increase execution time and consume more resources,
            leading to poor system performance.


3. What is optimization in computing?

Answer:

        (*) Optimization is the process of improving efficiency
             by minimizing time and resource consumption.
