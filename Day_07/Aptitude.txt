Ques-2

What is the probability of getting a number greater than 4 when rolling a dice?

Solution:
          
          probability possible  = {1,2,3,4,5,6} 

                          total = 6

          Numbers greater than 4 = {5,6} 
                      
                        favorable = 2

                 P = 2 / 6 
                   
                   = 1 / 3
​

Answer: 1/3 .

Ques-3

A bag contains 3 red balls and 2 blue balls.
Find probability of picking a red ball.

Solution:

         
          Total balls = 5

          Red balls = 3

          P (red ball) = 3 / 5


✅ Answer: 3/5 .
